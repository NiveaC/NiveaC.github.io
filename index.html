我的工作交會於產品策略、營運與跨部門執行。在我主導過的 SaaS 平台產品決策中，挑戰不只是 UX——更需要同時理解訂閱邏輯、付款行為、生命週期分眾與平台限制。My work sits at the intersection of product strategy, operations, and cross-functional execution. I've led product decisions on SaaS platforms where the challenge wasn't just UX — it was understanding subscription logic, payment behavior, lifecycle segmentation, and platform constraints all at once.

我不只找出使用者需要什麼，還會梳理解決方案背後的商業機制——為什麼可行、需要哪些營運調整、如何在不同團隊間排出執行序。I don't just identify what users need. I map the business mechanics behind why a solution is viable, what operational changes it requires, and how to sequence execution across teams.
